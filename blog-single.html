Vortex Games

Vortex Games is a company(team of developers) based in Bulgaria. Since our founding - May 2018 we working every day and trying to create our games better after every update! Its games are created for different devices - Tablets, Smart Phones, Play Stations, Computers etc. Until now we have released only two games, but we keep working!

How to download those games?

When our games are released they probably will be added to game stores like Google Play, App Store, Steam etc. You can subscribe for us below or join our Discord server to get notified when we make any update or release some game!

Games Price and Developer job

Vortex Games is a new company founded recently, untill now we havent uploaded any of our games in game stores, thats why we dont earned any funds or have a sponsors. Our games will not be free for now!

If you looking for developers job and you want to join our developers team you aways can contact us by the email below or via our discord server to get more information. We just wanna warn you, the payment will not be so high!
