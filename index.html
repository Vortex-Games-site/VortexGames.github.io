Our Story

Vortex Games is company founded since May 2018. We was working only on mobile games, but after Juny 2018 we will create games for every device like IOS, Android, PS4, Xbox, Computers and etc. Some of our games may need a payment. We made our games by Unity.

Reason our games may cost money - we dont have any sponsors right now, so we cant earn money from free games. Everyone like free games, so we will try to release maximal count of free games. We really sorry about payment for some of our games!
